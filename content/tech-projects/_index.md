        ### ART-Math (2023 – present)

        ![ART-Math screenshot](/images/projects/art-math.png)

        ART-Math is a story-telling-based math learning platform. It integrates the components of art so students can represent their mental modeling of math on canvas. Also, I integrated a generative AI-support for students' creative writing stage.

        ---

        ### [LEVI] ALTER-Math (2023 – present)

        ![ALTER-Math screenshot](/images/projects/alter-math.png)

        AI-augmented Learning by Teaching to Enhance and Renovate Math Learning (ALTER-Math) is a Large Language Model-powered teachable agent AI system that simulates a less knowledgeable student to promote learning-by-teaching. This paradigm shift of AI from being seen as an intelligent instructor to being taught as a naive student successfully engaged more than 1500 students across Florida.

        ---

        ### [NSF ITEST] Mflow

        ![Mflow screenshot](/images/projects/mflow.png)

        Mflow is a flow-based music programming technology that engages young children in learning computer science by creating music. Young students can have fun while learning about essential computational thinking concepts, such as loop and conditionals.

        Mflow is open to everyone: [https://mflow.sciencemusic.org/](https://mflow.sciencemusic.org/)
